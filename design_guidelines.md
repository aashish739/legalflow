# AAS Legal Website Design Guidelines

## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach**: Drawing inspiration from professional legal websites like Baker McKenzie and Latham & Watkins, emphasizing trust, expertise, and modern professionalism while maintaining the conservative nature expected in legal services.

## Core Design Elements

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Deep Navy: 220 85% 15% (trust, stability, legal authority)
- Charcoal: 220 10% 20% (professional text, headings)

**Secondary Colors:**
- Warm White: 45 20% 98% (clean backgrounds)
- Light Gray: 220 8% 95% (subtle sections)
- Gold Accent: 45 85% 60% (sparingly for CTAs and highlights)

**Dark Mode:**
- Background: 220 15% 8%
- Text: 220 10% 90%
- Cards: 220 12% 12%

### Typography
- **Primary**: Inter (Google Fonts) - clean, professional, excellent readability
- **Headings**: Inter 600-700 weight
- **Body**: Inter 400-500 weight
- **Legal Text**: Inter 300 weight for disclaimers

### Layout System
Consistent Tailwind spacing using units: **4, 8, 12, 16, 24** (p-4, m-8, gap-12, etc.)
- Generous whitespace for professional appearance
- Maximum content width: 1200px
- Grid system: 12-column for services, 3-column for team

### Component Library

**Navigation:**
- Clean horizontal navbar with firm logo
- Subtle hover states with underline animations
- Mobile hamburger menu with slide-out drawer

**Hero Section:**
- Large, professional hero image with overlay
- Centered text layout with firm tagline
- Two prominent CTAs: "Our Services" and "Contact Us"
- Buttons with blurred backgrounds when over hero image

**Content Cards:**
- Service cards with subtle shadows and hover elevation
- Partner profile cards with professional headshots
- Case study cards with brief descriptions

**Forms:**
- Clean contact forms with floating labels
- Professional styling with navy accents
- Clear validation states

## Images
**Hero Image**: Large professional image of a modern law office, courthouse, or business handshake - positioned as full-width background with dark overlay for text readability.

**Partner Photos**: Professional headshots in business attire, consistent lighting and background.

**Service Icons**: Simple, professional line icons representing legal services (scales of justice, contracts, etc.).

**Office Photos**: Clean, modern office spaces showcasing professionalism.

## Key Principles
- **Trust-focused**: Every element reinforces credibility and expertise
- **Information hierarchy**: Clear visual distinction between services, expertise areas, and contact information
- **Professional restraint**: Minimal animations, focus on content clarity
- **Mobile-responsive**: Ensuring accessibility across all devices for client convenience
